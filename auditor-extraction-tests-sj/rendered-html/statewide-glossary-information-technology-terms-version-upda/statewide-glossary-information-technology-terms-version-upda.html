Statewide Glossary of Information Technology Terms: Version Updates

Statewide IT Glossary

March 2025

Purpose

The purpose of the Glossary of Terms: Version Updates is to provide a central repository of updates to the Statewide Information Technology Glossary of Terms that apply to documentation created and maintained by the North Carolina Office of the State Chief Information Officer (State CIO).

Scope

This document covers all State information technology areas that fall under the responsibility of the State CIO. The updates apply to statewide information technology policies, standards, and the statewide architecture for all government agencies of the State of North Carolina.
