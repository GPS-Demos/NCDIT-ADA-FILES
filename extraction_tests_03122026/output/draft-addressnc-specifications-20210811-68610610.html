AddressNC Data Content Standard
Heading (H2)
Preface
Paragraph
The AddressNC Data Content Standard found in this document was compiled by the AddressNC Steering Committee from October 2020 through January 2021. The AddressNC Steering Committee was formed in June 2020 and began meeting monthly from July 2020 through January 2021. The steering committee consisted of representatives from both state and local government as well as the CURISA Addressing Group, the NC League of Municipalities, and the NC Association of County Commissioners. A full list of the members can be found in the linked member listing¹ within the References section of this document.
Heading (H2)
Introduction
Paragraph
This AddressNC Data Content Standard was compiled and proposed to the North Carolina (NC) Statewide Mapping Advisory Committee (SMAC) by the AddressNC Steering Committee that was formed in 2020. The AddressNC Steering Committee derived this standard from the NENA Standard for NG9-1-1 GIS Data Model², the National Address Database (NAD) Schema v2³, the FGDC Content Standard for Unites States Thoroughfare, Landmark, and Postal Address Data Standard⁴, as well as the previous two AddressNC data products that were published in 2009 and 2014. Considerations to support the various review of address data for census needs also took place.
Heading (H2)
Definition
Paragraph
The AddressNC Data Content Standard is designed as a digital representation of the addresses throughout North Carolina. Each digital address point contains attributes that describe specific characteristics of the actual address to identify features of that locations.
Heading (H2)
General Data Requirements
Heading (H3)
Coordinate System
Paragraph
The AddressNC data product will be published in the NC State Plane NAD83 (ESPG: 2264) coordinate reference system.
Heading (H3)
Geometry - Point Feature Type
Paragraph
The address data published for AddressNC will be a point feature geometry type.
Heading (H3)
Fields and Domains
Paragraph
A complete listing of the fields and their associated domains can be found in Appendix A. Where the domains have been defined by the State of North Carolina these values and definitions can be found within Appendix B.
Heading (H3)
Metadata Standards
Paragraph
The metadata format that will be used for the AddressNC data is the North Carolina State and Local Government Metadata Profile for Geospatial Data and Services. A link to the metadata profile document⁵ can be found within the References section of this document.
