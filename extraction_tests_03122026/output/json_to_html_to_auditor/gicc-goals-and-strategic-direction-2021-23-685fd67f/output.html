GICC Goals and Strategic Direction 2021-2023

N.C. Geographic Information Coordinating Council

April 8, 2020

Timeline

  • May Council Meeting – Discuss Goals and Strategic Direction
  • Summer 2021- Review and refinement
  • Tasking for Committees – Management and Operations Committee
  • August Council Meeting – Finalize and adopt

Goals

  • Improve, expand, and support statewide geospatial data and applications.
  • Collaborate and conduct outreach for more integration of geospatial data in information technology for expanded benefits in the geospatial community in North Carolina.
