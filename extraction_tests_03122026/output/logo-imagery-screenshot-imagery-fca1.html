Heading (H2)
How to register
List (ordered)
  1. Go to https://myncid.nc.gov/
  2. Click “Register now”.
List (ordered)
  1. Select the account type (Individual or business).
  2. Fill the required details with your information. Click SAVE
  3. Verify your details and click CONFIRM and you will see a Success message on the screen.
  4. Go to your email and click on the activation link to complete the registration.
Heading (H2)
How to unlock your account
Paragraph
**Note:** Your account will automatically unlock after 2 hours.
List (ordered)
  1. Go to https://myncid.nc.gov/
  2. Click “Trouble Signing On?”
List (ordered)
  1. Select the account type (Individual or business).
  2. Click “Unlock Account”.
List (ordered)
  1. Enter your username & click Next
  2. Select an option to receive the OTP (Email, Text message)
  3. Enter the OTP sent to you and click: Submit
Heading (H2)
How to retrieve your username
List (ordered)
  1. Go to https://myncid.nc.gov/
  2. Click “Trouble Signing On?”
List (ordered)
  1. Select an account type (Individual or business).
  2. Click “Forgot Username”.
List (ordered)
  1. Enter your email address, click: Send Request
  2. You will receive an email (and optionally, SMS) with your username.

Heading (H2)
How to enroll Open Subscription Applications
List (ordered)
  1. Go to
    • https://myncid.nc.gov/
  2. Login by entering your username and password.
  3. Go to the “Group” tab and click on the “ADD GROUP” button.
List (ordered)
  1. Select the group(s) you want to be part of and click on the “Save” button.
Heading (H2)
How to delete your account
List (ordered)
  1. Go to
    • https://myncid.nc.gov/
  2. Login by entering your username and password.
  3. Click on the ⋮ in the upper right corner and click on “Delete my account”.
List (ordered)
  1. Type “delete” to confirm and click on the “Delete” button.
