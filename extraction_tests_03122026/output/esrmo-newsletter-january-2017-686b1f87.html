Happy New Year! What will you resolve to do?
Paragraph
The ESRMO would like to share this monthly newsletter with information we hope you find beneficial. As we say farewell to the year 2016 and welcome a new year 2017, it is a good time to remind ourselves of *our shared responsibility* to keep the State of NC safe from cyber threats. Some resolutions we could make for this year are as follows: to use strong passwords for all systems; to recognize suspicious email and avoid phishing attempts by not clicking on links in them; to not download or install unauthorized software; to avoid connecting to a public Wi-Fi; and to use secure methods to store and transmit sensitive State data. Together, we can help make the State of NC a safer place to conduct business online.
