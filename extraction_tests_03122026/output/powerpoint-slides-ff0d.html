Alex Talarico 5/21 11:53 AM For your scenario, which is to move to SPO, your report server files will no longer be hosted on SharePoint - instead you'd need to move to PBI server on premises fist with the scripts I mention, and then jump these from there into PBI online.. even then, stored passwords don't come across, schedules and other settings may not come across ... with all the manual steps and dev requirements, the best option - in my honest opinion - would be to upload the report files to PBI Online and re-configure, republish, nor recreate any of the things that don't work when tested - simple reports come across relatively well, but in some cases it's a full rebuild for complex reports. This is because some reports have sub-reports or other linking mechanisms that just don't migrate well, and instead it'd be less effort to rebuild with newer tech.
