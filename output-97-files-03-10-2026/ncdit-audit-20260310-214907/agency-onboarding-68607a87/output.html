How Does My Agency Get CJLEADS?

Step 1

Kickoff Event with CJLEADS Team will provide overview of on-boarding process

Step 2: Execution of License and Usage Agreement

Step 2

Execution of License and Usage Agreement

CJLEADS Team provides Information Access Policy and License & User Agreement

Requesting Agency:

  1. Identifies & provides Signatory Authority
  2. Identifies internal points of contact
  3. Identifies authorized security roles
  4. Signs License and Usage Agreement
  5. Files signed documents with CJLEADS Team

Step 3

CJLEADS Team will:

  1. Meet with Agency NCID Administrator
  2. Review and Refine Div/Sect for Agency

Is requesting Agency an established NCID Organization? (Yes/No)

If No:

ITS and CJLEADS Team will:

  1. Assist Agency with adding Org/Div/Sect
  2. Agency Admin obtains Org NCID

Step 4

CJLEADS adds User Admins to CJLEADS UA Tool

Step 5

CJLEADS Trains User Admins

  1. NCID Administration
  2. CJLEADS User Admin

Step 6

Agency Admins Establish User NCID Accts and adds new CJLEADS Users

Step 7

CJLEADS and Agency schedules and trains new CJLEADS Users (Accounts activated at the end of training class)
