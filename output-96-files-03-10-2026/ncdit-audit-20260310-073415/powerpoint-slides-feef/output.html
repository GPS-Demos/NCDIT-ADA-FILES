Alt text

Alt text is a property of an image that allows screen readers to describe the image.

Guidelines for alt text:

  • Concise
  • Include any relevant text in the image
  • Reflects intention and context of the image
  • May be left blank if image is purely decorative or redundant
  • Confirm any auto generated alt text

Everything you need to know to write effective alt text

Bonus Tip: This also applies to images in email, Word, PowerPoint, PDF, and other documents.

Color contrast & font size

High contrast colors make content easier to read for everyone, and specifically people with low vision and people who identify as neurodivergent.

Ensure font size is readable (12-14 points)

Captions and transcripts

Provide captions for all video content

Offer transcripts for audio content

Convey information

Avoid conveying information with color alone which is difficult for people with color blindness.

In Teams, each different state has a symbol (or no symbol, in the case of busy).

Accessible links

Describe the link destination clearly

Avoid generic phrases like “click here”

Emojis and GIFs
